Our Story

Founded in 2018, Fresh Squeeze began with a simple mission: to make nutritious, delicious juices accessible to everyone. What started as a small farmer's market stand has grown into a beloved community staple.

We believe that what you put into your body matters. That's why we source only the finest organic produce and use cold-press technology to preserve vital nutrients and enzymes.
